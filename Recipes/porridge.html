Potato Porridge

Home

Description

An easy Potato Porridge recipe that needs simple ingredients, gets ready in a few minutes and is perfect for lunch or midweek dinner.

Potatoes are a great substitute for african yams in many recipes and this luscious potato pottage is one of them.

”A Yummy Yam Porridge Recipe substitute for when those African Yams turn to GOLD.”

Ingredients

  • Potato of your choice
  • Crayfish Powder
  • Chicken Bouillon Powder
  • Red Ball Pepper
  • Green Ball Pepper (Optional)
  • Onions
  • Palm Oil (can be substituted for with vegetable oil)

Steps

  1. Add water to cubed potatoes, turn on heat, add chopped onions and cook till tender.
  2. Blend red ball peppers, heat up oil and sautee onions.
  3. Add blended ball peppers to sauteed onions, cook then add to cooked potatoes.
